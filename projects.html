FLOW SHIBA GAME Worked in team to create a game where the player, using the character of a shiba inu in a UFO, follows the frequency waves of a song of their choosing with arrow keys/wasd, to move up, slow down, move down or speed up. The closer they follow the wave, the more points they get. If the player fall out of the range of the wave, or hits it, then the game ends.
